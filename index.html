Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to clean up and automate my business data?

Most projects are a fixed price between $2,500 and $5,000, scoped up front so you know the number before any work starts. That covers cleaning up your data, automating one or two manual workflows, and setting up a clear dashboard. Ongoing support, if you want it, is an optional $300–$750/month.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Small and mid-sized businesses (roughly 2–30 people) drowning in spreadsheets and disconnected apps, plus small nonprofits with the same problem. Two exceptions: medical/dental (HIPAA) and defense/aerospace (ITAR/CMMC). Everyone else is fair game.

What if we're not ready for AI yet?

That's fine — AI is the last step, not the first. Every project starts with cleaning up your data, because you can't automate or query data that isn't clean and organized.

Do we need to buy software licenses?

No. The whole stack (PostgreSQL, NocoDB, n8n, Caddy) is open source and self-hosted. You pay for a small server (about $9/month) and our time — nothing else.

Can we keep our data on-site?

Yes. We can run the identical system on a mini-PC at your location and manage it remotely — ideal for sensitive customer, financial, or donor records.

What's included in the monthly support?

A monthly check-in, backup monitoring, quarterly updates, and workflow improvements. New features or integrations are scoped separately as add-ons.

How long does a typical project take?

A typical project runs 2 to 4 weeks from cleanup through setup, depending on how messy the data is. Adding reporting and AI on top can extend that. Optional support starts after handoff.

What happens to our data if we stop working together?

You own your data. At any point you can request a full export in standard format, readable by any database tool. No vendor lock-in, ever.

What's the first step?

A free systems review. No pitch and no proposal until we understand what you actually have and what you actually need.
